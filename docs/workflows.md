# Workflows

This page provides an overview of the **key workflows** within Clarity Docs. It is designed to guide users through standard processes and best practices.

---

## 1. Overview

Workflows help ensure consistency and efficiency across the organization. They define the **steps, responsibilities, and timelines** for common processes.

---

## 2. Example Workflows

Here are a few example workflows you might find in the organization:

1. **Document Approval Workflow**  
   - Create or update a document  
   - Submit for review  
   - Approver reviews and either approves or requests changes  
   - Document is published once approved  

2. **Incident Reporting Workflow**  
   - Employee reports an incident  
   - Safety officer reviews and categorizes the report  
   - Investigation is conducted  
   - Corrective actions are implemented  

3. **Training & Onboarding Workflow**  
   - New employee is assigned mandatory training  
   - Completion is tracked in the system  
   - Feedback is collected and reviewed  

---

## 3. Best Practices

- Always follow the documented steps in each workflow.  
- Keep records of approvals, incidents, and training completions.  
- Review workflows regularly to identify areas for improvement.  

---

## 4. Getting Started

- Use the sidebar to navigate to other workflows or related documentation.  
- Admins can update workflows directly by editing `.md` files in GitHub.  
- Ensure all updates are reviewed and tested before publishing.
