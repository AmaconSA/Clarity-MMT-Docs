# Health & Safety

This page provides an overview of the **Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) policies and procedures** within Clarity Docs.

---

## 1. Introduction

Health and safety are critical to maintaining a safe working environment. This page outlines general guidance and procedures to **protect employees, contractors, and visitors**.

---

## 2. Key Policies

- **Workplace Safety** – Follow all safety rules, wear protective equipment, and report hazards immediately.  
- **Emergency Procedures** – Know emergency exits, evacuation routes, and contact points for incidents.  
- **Incident Reporting** – Report accidents, near-misses, or unsafe conditions promptly.  
- **Training & Awareness** – Complete all mandatory health and safety training programs.

---

## 3. Example Procedures

1. **Incident Reporting Procedure**  
   - Identify and document the incident  
   - Notify the supervisor or safety officer  
   - Conduct investigation and corrective action  
   - Record the outcome in the system  

2. **Fire Safety Procedure**  
   - Know the location of fire extinguishers and alarms  
   - Follow evacuation routes  
   - Participate in fire drills regularly  

3. **Equipment Safety Checks**  
   - Inspect tools and machinery before use  
   - Ensure safety guards and personal protective equipment (PPE) are in place  
   - Report any faults or hazards immediately

---

## 4. Best Practices

- Always follow documented procedures  
- Encourage a culture of safety and awareness  
- Continuously review and improve safety measures  
- Maintain clear records of training, incidents, and audits
